History of Bhairavakona Gangamma Thalli Temple

🏛️ Temple History

For over 150 years, the village of Kothapalli has been the sacred home of Sri Gangamma Thalli. For generations, she has been worshipped not only by the local villagers but by people from all the nearby villages. For many, she is their cherished family deity, a powerful mother who protects their health and brings prosperity.Located on the peaceful road to the famous Bhairavakona temple, her shrine has been a spiritual stop for pilgrims for over a century. Over time, heavy rains and floods sadly damaged the old temple structure. However, while the building was weakened, the faith of the families who worship her remained as strong as ever.To honor her 150-year history, we have taken the initiative to rebuild her temple. This new home for the Goddess features beautiful traditional South Indian architecture and intricate stone carvings. We are restoring this holy site so that future generations can continue to seek her blessings.We invite you to join us for the grand Opening Ceremony (April 24th–26th, 2026). Come and be a part of this special moment as we celebrate the rebirth of our Mother’s sacred home.
