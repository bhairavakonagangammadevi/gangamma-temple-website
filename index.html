About Sri Sri Sri Gangamma Thalli Temple

Sri Gangammathalli Temple is being constructed in the serene village of Kothapalli, Ambavaram mandal, Markoporum District, Andhra Pradesh. This sacred shrine is dedicated to Goddess Gangamma Thalli, the powerful protector deity who blesses devotees with health, prosperity, and spiritual growth.

With over 150 years of spiritual heritage and significance, the new idol inauguration ceremony (24-04-2026 to 26-04-2026) marks a historic milestone. The temple features traditional South Indian architecture with intricate carvings and a majestic gopuram (tower).

  • ✨ Traditional South Indian Architecture
  • 🏯 Majestic Gopuram & Intricate Carvings
  • 📿 150 Years of Spiritual Heritage
  • 🙏 Dedicated to Goddess Gangamma - Protector Deity
  • 💰 Health, Prosperity & Spiritual Growth
  • 🤝 Community Blessing & Devotion
